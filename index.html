How to play

Current Prototype Rules

Win the round

Place the element that leaves every tile on the board fully bonded. Complete the Formula to claim the round. Win two rounds to win the duel.

Bonding

  1. The first element enters at the center.
  2. Every later element must touch at least one tile with open potency.
  3. An element cannot touch a fully bonded tile.
  4. It cannot touch more open neighbors than its potency.
  5. It bonds automatically with every eligible neighbor.
Contribute

Place an element. Its current potency locks. Every other element in your hand decays by 1.

Extract

Decay your active hand by 1, then place a fresh potency-4 element from your pool into an empty live slot.

Observe

Decay every active element in your hand by 2.

Collapse

An element that reaches 0 is lost and collapses its slot permanently. If all three of your slots collapse, you lose the round.
