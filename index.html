Rules

Win the round

Force your opponent into a position where they cannot take a legal action. The player who Forfeits loses the round, and the other player claims the Formula. Win two rounds to win the game.

Continue the session

A game is best of 3 rounds. A match is best of 3 games. A session is best of 3 matches. Only fully bonded tiles score by default. The game winner banks their unsecured Formula points and may add one shared Discovery.

Bonding

  1. The first tile enters at the center.
  2. Every later tile must touch at least one tile with open potency.
  3. A tile cannot touch a fully bonded tile.
  4. It cannot touch more open neighbors than its potency.
  5. It bonds automatically with every eligible neighbor.
Contribute

Place a tile. Its current potency locks. Every other tile in your hand decays by 1.

Extract

Decay your active hand by 1, then place a fresh potency-4 tile from your pool into an empty live slot.

Forfeit

Concede the round. If Contribute and Extract are both unavailable, Forfeit is your only base action.

Collapse

A tile that reaches 0 is lost and collapses its slot. A collapsed hand does not end the round automatically. The affected player must Forfeit unless a discovered skill gives them another legal action.

Named Formulas

Only a fully fulfilled board can receive a Formula name. Open Formulas may still be claimed, but they are unfulfilled and cannot be cataloged or named.

Shared discoveries

The game winner may spend banked points on one skill between games. Every purchased skill changes the rules for both players for the rest of the session.
